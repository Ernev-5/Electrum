   **A modern, lightweight, client-side optimization modpack built on Sodium for Fabric.** 

Welcome to Electrum! If you are looking to remove stuttering, boost your FPS, and smooth out your gameplay without changing the core vanilla experience, you're in the right place.

This pack is designed to deliver **maximum performance** with a minimal footprint, optimizing rendering, memory management, and game logic altogether.

##   🤔 Why Choose Electrum?

Unlike other modpacks bloated with redundant or unnecessary mods, Electrum focuses strictly on the **essentials**. By cutting out the clutter, it ensures **excellent compatibility** across a wide range of devices: from low-end laptops to high-end gaming pcs, and allows for lightning-fast updates to the latest Minecraft versions.

Plus, it serves as the perfect foundation: install it to get flawless performance first, then easily add your favorite mods right on top!

##  ⭐ Key Features:

  - **Maximum Performance**: Powered by Sodium and a carefully curated selection of optimization mods, it offers by design **the best possible performance** for all the devices: you'll experience a dramatic **FPS boost** and significantly **reduced loading times**.
  
-  **Ultra-Lightweight**: No **unnecessary** or **intrusive** quality-of-life mods. The game stays **100% true to Vanilla** in both looks and mechanics.
  
- **Universal Compatibility**: As a purely **client-side** modpack, it doesn't add any custom blocks or items. You can use it in any **single-player world** or join any **multiplayer server**.

- **Pre-configured**: All included mods are thoroughly **tested** and **fine-tuned** to work in perfect harmony, **preventing crashes** right out of the box.
-  **Open-source**: The modpack is fully open-source and available on [github](https://github.com/dashboard)
